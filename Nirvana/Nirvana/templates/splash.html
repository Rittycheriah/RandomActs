Competition. Kindness. Can you think of a game that combines the two? In this game, the point is to make the best of humanity combine with our basic need for competition. Log your acts, get points, and share with friends. Start your journey from Grasshopper to Nirvana today!

How It Works

Did you give your friend a compliment? Did you go out of your way to help that stranger get directions? Log it and you gain points!

  1. Log your act.
  2. Comment and like other users acts to help them gain points.
  3. As you gain points, you will increase in rank. With each increase in points, you are one step closer to attaining Nirvana
  4. To see where you compare, go to the Leaderboard page to view the top users and see where you are
